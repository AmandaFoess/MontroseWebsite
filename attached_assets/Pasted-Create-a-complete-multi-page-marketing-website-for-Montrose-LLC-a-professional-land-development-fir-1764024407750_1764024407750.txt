🛠 Services Page
Hero Section

Main Tagline (H1): Our Services

Subheading (H3):
Comprehensive land development services tailored to meet the unique needs of the project.

Full Services List (Each with Title + Description + Icon)

Land Acquisition
Market studies/competitive market analysis, pro forma development, initial site assessment.

Site Evaluation & Due Diligence
Land suitability review, risk identification, budget estimates, project milestone creation, environmental studies, project scope statement, project requirements statement, identify stakeholders.

Conceptual Site Planning
Initial sketch plan development with roads, buildings, utilities, and stormwater based on project vision identifying.

Land Use Analysis & Rezoning
Zoning analysis, municipal coordination, community meetings/stakeholder engagement, and public hearing support to achieve successful rezoning.

Engineering & Construction Docs
Engineering plan oversight, regulatory coordination, value engineering, documentation, project specification development.

Permitting & Entitlements
Permit review tracking, agency coordination, comment resolution, and approval management.

Pre-Construction
Budget updates, contractor RFPs, proposal review, schedule alignment before construction, resource selection for work packages.

Site Construction
On-site meeting management, work tracking, issue resolution, contractor invoice review, documenting progress, ensuring work meets plans/specs.

Vertical Construction
Site visits to monitor vertical build progress and document any on-site damages.

Close Out
Permit closure, bond release, final documentation delivery, agency coordination, utility as-built coordination, public infrastructure turnover, BMP conversions.
